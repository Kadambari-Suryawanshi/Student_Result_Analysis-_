About the College

Government Polytechnic Dhule was established in the year 1960. The campus of the institute is spread across the 52 acres of land with academic and administrative building, Workshop, Girls Hostels, Boys Hostels, Faculty and staff quarters, Play Ground and Multi-Purpose Hall with Guest House. The Institute is located at Vidyanagari, Deopur, Dhule on the Bay of Panjara River on the north side of Dhule, 4 km from central bus stand. In a well-planned city, the town planning of the city was designed by a notable engineer Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, awarded by Bharat Ratna. Initially diploma course in Civil engineering and Mechanical engineering was introduced in 1960, now the Institute runs five full time regular three years AICTE approved diploma courses.

Vision

To generate knowledge oriented, technically well-equipped and visionary human resources who are majority from rural sector to meet ever changing industrial challenges in particular and society in general at large.

Mission

To ensure and to facilitate well-coordinated educational resources and productive learning resources, in terms of cognitive material inputs and infrastructure so as to transform matriculated adolescent into all round developed, morally-ethically self-righteous individuals who have competitive knowledge and trained technical skill-set with themselves where such intermediate technical manpower will confidently face each and every challenge given to them by current time, industrial circumstances and changing scenario of society.
