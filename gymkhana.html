Gymkhana
The activities under Gymkhana are divided into two parts Sports and Cultural Activities
    Sports:

    Every year students secretaries are nominated by the Gymkhana for the institute and also the class Representative (CR) for individual classes the talents of students in the area of sports are encouraged and they are given participation in the 'c' zone and in the state level later diploma sports competitive to represent the institute. Best awards are also given by Gymkhana.

      Sports Equipment And Play Ground:

      Kits of Cricket, Hockey, Football, Weight lifting gym, Carrom, Chess Board, Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, Badminton, Basket ball, Volley ball, ground is available.

        Cultural Activities:

        These include programmes related to art & culture. Activities like singing, painting, debating, quiz, essay writing & elocution competitions, celebration of anniversaries of great personalities, blood donation camp, tree plantation etc are organized under this forum.

            Annual Prize Distribution Ceremony:

            (Contact person: Vice - President, Gathering) The most popular among the cultural activities is the Annual Prize Distribution Ceremony in which the students enjoy & share their talent in art. It is a grand event where students qualities are reflected in the form of their artistic as well as organizational qualities. Gymkhana also provides financial support for student welfare activities.
